## 2. What the service is

Strix is a goal-tracking application. You describe a goal in plain language, an AI assistant interviews you about your starting point and constraints, and the system generates a structured plan: daily habits, weekly sessions, milestones, and equipment with deadlines. Your active goals roll up into a single daily view.

Every AI-generated plan and every AI-proposed change is presented to you for review before it takes effect. You accept, edit, or reject it. Nothing is applied to your goals silently.

## 4. AI-generated content — important disclaimer

Plans, tasks, milestones, schedules, intensity calibrations, and equipment suggestions in Strix are generated by an artificial-intelligence model from the information you provide. Read this section carefully; it is central to how you should use the service.

- **AI output is a suggestion, not advice.** Nothing the service generates is medical advice, professional training or coaching advice, legal advice, financial advice, or any other form of professional advice. AI-generated plans are a starting point that you adapt with your own judgment and, where appropriate, professional guidance.
- **Physical goals carry real risk.** Many goals on Strix involve physical exertion — mountaineering, running, climbing, strength training. Before beginning any physical training program, consult a physician or qualified professional, particularly if you have a medical condition, an injury history, or are new to the activity. An AI-generated training plan cannot account for everything about your body and circumstances.
- **Safety flags can be overridden — at your own decision.** During intake, the AI assesses whether your stated goal and timeline are safe and realistic. If it identifies meaningful risk, it explains the concern and proposes a safer alternative. You may override that concern and continue. If you do, you proceed on your own judgment and at your own risk; the AI's concern and your override are recorded with the goal.
- **Equipment suggestions are informational.** For safety-critical gear (for example, mountaineering or climbing equipment), verify suitability, fit, and certification with a qualified professional or retailer before relying on it.
- **No guarantee of outcomes.** Strix does not promise that you will reach any goal, by any date, at any level of performance.
- **AI output can be wrong.** AI models make mistakes — including confident-sounding ones. Review every plan before accepting it.

### 5.2 Free-tier usage limits

On the Free tier:

- **3 plan generations per calendar month** (creating a new goal's plan or fully regenerating one).
- **2 plan adjustments (replans) per calendar month.** Weekly check-ins are always available; the AI proposes changes at most twice per month.
- **Unlimited intake conversations.**

Counters reset on the first day of each calendar month in your account's timezone. When you reach a cap, the action does not queue and is not silently skipped — you can wait for the reset or upgrade. Pro and Max have no usage caps.

### 5.3 Free trial (Max only)

- We offer a **one-week free trial of the Max tier**. Pro has no trial.
- **A payment card is required up front** to start the trial.
- **If you have not canceled by the end of the trial week, your card is charged** and your subscription continues on Max at the price you selected.
- **If you cancel during the trial, you are not charged.** You keep Max access until the end of the trial week, after which your account moves to the Free tier.
- If, at the end of a canceled trial, you have more active goals than the Free tier allows, you choose which goals to keep active on the selection screen described in §5.4. The goals you do not keep are archived — preserved in full, not deleted.

### 5.4 Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from billing settings.

- **One step, no hurdles.** If your active goals fit within the destination tier's limit, cancellation is a single confirmation. If you have more active goals than the destination tier allows, you are shown a single selection screen to choose which goals stay active; completing that screen completes the cancellation. That screen is the only step — there are no additional confirmation screens, no retention offers, and no requirement to contact support.
- **Paid periods run their course.** When you cancel a paid subscription, it stays active until the end of the period you have already paid for, then your account moves to the Free tier. When you cancel a trial, access continues until the trial week ends (§5.3).
- **Archived goals are preserved, not deleted.** Archiving is a status change. All tasks, milestones, equipment, completion history, and intake records are kept and can be restored if you later return to a tier with room for them.
- After cancellation we send one confirmation email. We do not send recurring win-back or retention emails.

### 5.5 Refunds

- **Monthly subscriptions are not refundable.**
- **Annual subscriptions are eligible for a prorated refund within 30 days of purchase**, requested from billing settings. After 30 days, annual fees are not refundable.
- If you delete your account while an annual subscription is within its 30-day refund window, the prorated refund is issued automatically as part of deletion.
- This policy is also displayed in the app's billing settings.

### 5.6 Price changes

We may change prices. If we do, we will give you advance notice, and the new price applies no earlier than your next renewal. If you do not accept a price change, cancel before it takes effect.
